Projects

AI Trace Finder

AI Trace Finder is a digital forensic project designed to identify the source scanner of a scanned image using artificial intelligence techniques. The system analyzes unique device-specific artifacts such as sensor pattern noise, pixel-level distortions, and compression signatures that are unintentionally embedded during the scanning process. By extracting these hidden noise patterns and applying machine learning algorithms, the model learns to differentiate between scanners and accurately predict the originating device of an unknown image. This project demonstrates the application of image processing, feature extraction, and classification models in cybersecurity and digital forensics. AI Trace Finder can be used in fraud detection, document authentication, and legal investigations where verifying the authenticity and origin of scanned documents is critical.
